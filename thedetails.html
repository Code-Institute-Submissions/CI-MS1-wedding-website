How to get there!

Airplane

You fly to Hakodate Airport from Haneda Airport in Tokyo and take a quick 10 min taxi ride straight to Imagine Hotel (It is also possible to fly from Sapporo Airport). We recommend you book the flight from Haneda with Air Do, they have a special price for tourists.

Train

You can take the Shinkansen all the way from Tokyo Station to Hakodate. The entire one way trip costs around 23,500 yen and takes under 5 hours. When you arrive to Hakodate you can either take a taxi or a bus from the train station to Imagine Hotel.

Bus

There is also the option of taking a night bus from Tokyo to Hakodate, this will take quite a long time so we do recommend the other means of transportation. If you want to take this journey, contact us and we can help you to find information.
